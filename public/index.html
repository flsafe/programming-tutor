Quickly, Tell Me What Blueberry Tree Is

Blueberry Tree is a programming interview practice tool. We are the programming interview that is on your side! Have you ever been rejected in a programming interview with no feedback? Well we want to give you that feedback!

Blueberry Tree lets you practice solving programming interview exercises. Depending on how you do on an exercise Blueberry Tree will recommend easier exercises that let you get up to speed or recommend more challenging exercises to increase your programming skills.
